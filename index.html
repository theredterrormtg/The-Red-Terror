✦ My Story ✦
🎴

How it started

My MTG playgroup gave me the nickname The Red Terror — and it stuck. Over the years I've found real joy in buying and selling singles, Secret Lairs, collector boxes, and all things MTG.

I am currently selling on TCGPlayer, but the fees they take feel like money that could be doing something better in the world. So I decided to create my own path — a shop where those fees go to charity instead.

♥

Giving back

10% of every sale is donated to KaIND Giving — a nonprofit dedicated to inspiring kindness in our communities.

KaIND Giving supports cancer survivors, frontline healthcare workers, and spreads joy through random acts of kindness. Every purchase you make here helps make that possible.

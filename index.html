✦ My Story ✦
🃏

How it started

My Magic: The Gathering playgroup gave me the name The Red Terror — and it stuck. Over the years I've found real joy in buying and selling singles, Secret Lairs, collector boxes, and all things MTG.

I was selling on TCGPlayer, but the fees they take felt like money that could be doing something better in the world. So I decided to create my own path — a shop where those fees go to charity instead.

♥

Giving back

10% of every sale is donated to KaIND Giving — a nonprofit dedicated to inspiring kindness in our communities.

KaIND Giving supports cancer survivors, frontline healthcare workers, and spreads joy through random acts of kindness. Every card you buy here helps make that possible.

ⓘ One-time Firebase setup needed
To save listings to the cloud (so they appear on your live site), you need a free Firebase account.

1. Go to console.firebase.google.com → create a project called red-terror-shop
2. Click Firestore Database → Create database → Start in test mode
3. Click Storage → Get started
4. Go to Project Settings → copy your firebaseConfig object
5. Open this HTML file in a text editor and replace the FIREBASE_CONFIG_HERE placeholder near the bottom with your config

Until then, listings save to your browser only (still works for testing!).
